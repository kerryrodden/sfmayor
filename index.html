Ranked-choice voting in the election for SF mayor

London Breed had a strong lead in first-place votes, but Mark Leno almost closed the gap thanks to ranked-choice votes transferred from Jane Kim supporters. Leno and Kim had campaigned together for a 1-2 ranking.

Includes only the last 5 rounds of the ranked-choice process—after candidates Michelle Bravo and Amy Farah Weiss are eliminated.
